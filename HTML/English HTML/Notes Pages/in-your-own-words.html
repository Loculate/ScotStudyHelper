In Your Own Words

Video Credit to LHS English Department

In your own words questions make up a large portion of the RUAE paper, and are probably, the easiest marks to score during an exam. They only require a small sentence per mark, and are simple to answer correctly if you know what you are doing.

To answer an In Your Own Words question, you simply need to find a quote which links to the question given, and reword it in a way that it means the same thing, but without including any words from the original quote. For example, if I had the quote "It is through embracing our imperfections that we cultivate resilience and authenticity" I could reword it to "By accepting our flaws we can create strength and sincerity.", and then repeat this for however many marks the question is worth
